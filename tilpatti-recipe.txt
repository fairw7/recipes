Tilpatti Recipe

Ingredients:

1/2 cup of jaggery
1/2 cup of sesame seeds

How to make Tilpatti:

Grate jaggery into thin shreds
Take a flat pan and put on the stove
Mix jaggery and sesame seeds together
Once pan is heated, try to put the mixture in the center of the pan and start spreading slowly across the pan.
Jaggery would start liquifying slowly in the head.
Make a thick layer like an omlette and allow to bit solidfy for couple of mins.
Take out the Tilpatti crust from the pan.
